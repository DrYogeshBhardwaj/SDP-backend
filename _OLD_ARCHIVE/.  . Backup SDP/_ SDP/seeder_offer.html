What is a Seeder?

A responsible guide for the digital wellness journey.

This is NOT an MLM (Multi-Level Marketing) or Network Marketing program.

Overview

A Seeder is a participant who understands SDP and helps introduce the concept of Digital Pause to others in a responsible manner.

Role of a Seeder

  • Explains correct use of SDP
  • Builds a small, informed user group
  • Helps spread awareness about digital wellness

Payout & Transparency

  • All payouts are manual
  • Admin verification is required
  • Payments are processed via UPI or bank by the Admin
  • There is no automatic or guaranteed income

Important Statement

Becoming a Seeder is voluntary.

This is not a job offer, investment plan, or income scheme.
