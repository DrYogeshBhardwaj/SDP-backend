Using SDP as a User

Everything you need to know about Digital Pause

What is Digital Pause?

A Digital Pause is a short, guided break that helps your eyes and mind disengage from continuous screen usage.
It is not meditation, not therapy, and not religious—just a simple pause.

How SDP Works

Choose a short break based on your need:

⚡

2 Minutes

Quick Reset. A fast pause to relax eyes and release screen tension.

🌊

5 Minutes

Balance Break. Helps restore focus and reduce digital fatigue.

🌌

10 Minutes

Deep Pause. A longer disengagement for extended screen exposure.

Each break uses calm visuals and sound patterns designed for screen relief.
