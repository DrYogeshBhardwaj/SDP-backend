Partnering with SDP

SDP allows individuals to participate as partners by introducing others to healthier digital habits.

What a Partner Does

📢

Shares SDP with others

🌍

Helps build awareness about Digital Pause

🤝

Supports users in understanding short screen breaks

Earnings & Payouts

Any benefit sharing is handled manually and transparently by the system administrator.
This platform does not automate payments or collect money online.

Important Note

SDP is not an investment scheme.
There are no guarantees or promises of income.
Participation is voluntary and activity-based.
