What is SDP?

SDP (Sinaank Digital Pause) is a digital wellness system that reduces screen fatigue using special color and sound combinations.

⚠️ Note: Every mobile number has its own unique color and sound combination, so a different kit is required for every new mobile number.

How to use SDP

  • Click on "Start Break"
  • Choose a 2 / 5 / 10 minute break
  • Stay away from the screen and experience the sound and color
  • Return to your work with a fresh mind after the break

New: Break Alarm & Reminder

Set a reminder for your next break. The browser tab will flash "🔔 TIME FOR BREAK!" even if you are on another website, ensuring you never miss a session.
